About

Not a corporate. Just a Northlander with skills and a mission.

I'm Robert. 57, born and raised in Northland, and I've spent more of my life in community halls than behind a desk. I'm not an agency. No sales team, no account manager, no three-month waitlist.

What I have is a set of tech skills I've spent years quietly developing, a faith that keeps me grounded, and a simple conviction: Northland's small businesses, churches, and community organisations deserve quality online presence without having to sell internal organs to afford it.

I named this business after my daughter Merania — everyone knew her as Medz. I lost her to cervical cancer in August 2025. She was tough. Tenacious. Funny, strong-willed, kind, and always thinking of others. That's the standard I hold myself to.

If you're a real business run by a real person with a real budget, you're exactly who I built this for.
